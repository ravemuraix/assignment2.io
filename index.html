Asian Vegetables

Choy is the Chinese word for any leafy vegetable. The names of Asian vegetables are called different names in different areas of China, and thus may seem confusing

(source: https://www.vegetables.co.nz/vegetables-a-z/asian-greens)


There are hundreds of varieties of Asian vegetables. However, for immigrants or expats, not all vegetables and their names are translatable. This presents an issue for them who shop and consume Asian vegetables in an Asian market. Thus, we shall explore some commonly found vegetables in our website.
